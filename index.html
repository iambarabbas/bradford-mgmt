Meet Beau Bradford

I'm a second-generation San Diegan who grew up splitting time between University City (near La Jolla) and the mountains of Big Bear Lake, where my parents bought their first vacation home in 1978.

I learned property management early, tagging along with mom and dad as they managed their own properties and completed renovations. After my father passed in 1995, I took on more responsibility while attending San Diego State, graduating with honors in Integrated Marketing.

After years of corporate experience negotiating telecom easements for Verizon, AT&T, and others, I formalized our family business as Bradford Management in 2016. Today, I bring 25+ years of property experience and sharp negotiating skills to every client relationship.

I live in the Ivins, UT area with my wife Susan and our daughter Nashellie. When I'm not managing properties, you'll find me leading worship at Barabbas Road Church, working on classic cars, or enjoying the outdoors.
